Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 5, 2026

DayJobDevelopment LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates dayjob.dev and related services. This policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

We collect information in two ways:

  • Information you provide directly — when you contact us via email (e.g., matt@dayjob.dev), we receive your email address and the content of your message.
  • Usage data — we use Plausible Analytics (self-hosted at traffic.dayjob.dev) to collect anonymous, aggregate visitor statistics. Plausible does not use cookies and does not collect personally identifiable information. No IP addresses are stored. No cross-site tracking occurs.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiries and fulfill consulting engagements.
  • To understand how visitors use this site so we can improve it (via anonymous analytics only).

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies. Our analytics provider (Plausible) is cookieless by design.

Email Communications

If you email us, we retain that correspondence to manage our business relationship. You may request deletion of your email records at any time by contacting us.

Data Retention

We retain email correspondence for as long as it is relevant to an active or potential business relationship. Anonymous analytics data is retained indefinitely in aggregate form only.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you by emailing matt@dayjob.dev. We will respond within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy
